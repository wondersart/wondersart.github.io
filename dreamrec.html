About the Artist

Creating worlds never seen before and the constant return to nature as the ultimate designer and source of inspiration are the prosperous obsessions shaping the artistic practice of Silviu Vișan a.k.a Dreamrec. Growing up around his artisan father who was building oversized toys for an amusement park turned into the perfect context to learn about the emotional experience of immersive environments and the secrets of morphing a place into an event. Not to mention it was the starting point for the self-thought wizard to try his hand at DIY magic either bringing joy or conjuring horror.

Years later, using videofeedback and layers of state-of-the-art software as tools to explore the unusual time&space where computers become genuinely unpredictable and the output becomes a fluid stream of pareidolia, Dreamrec is channeling in international festivals, clubs and art spaces his own visual aesthetic: immersive, mesmerizing and fictional as if visitors / partygoers physically stepped into another dimension.

Video performances, interventions and large scale mapping, collaborative installations at V&A Museum, London (UK), Biennial of Young artists, Bucharest (RO), “Decenter Armory at Abrons Arts Center, NY ( USA), art & music festivals and clubs: Rokolectiv Festival, Bucharest (RO), TodaysArt Festival Hague (NL), ICAS Suite by Club Transmediale, Berlin (DE), Periferias Festival, Huesca (ES), Simultan Festival, Timisoara (RO), EasternDaze Night, Zilina (SK), Crack Festival, Rome (IT), Insomnia Festival, Tromso (NO), Arma17, Moscow (RU), Liquidroom Tokyo (JP) , Fabric, London (UK) etc.

Among Dreamrec’s long-term collaborations we would mention the ones with RPR Soundystem of the acclaimed RO-minimal music label [a:rpia:r] and with experimental musician working under Rochite and Sillyconductor monikers.

Sɛntɪənt

Price: 7.700 Euro

A biomorphic work generated with custom video-feedback algorithms, 'sentient' has a strong aesthetic link with something that Dreamrec calls the elegance of decay. Right within a rectangular shape, a surreal organic pattern is bubbling destruction with such forthright clarity that it makes us wonder if this is just a sample of a macro reality.
Dreamrec

Ambiguous beams

Price: 1.500 Euro

The initial sci-fi, post-apocalyptic character of a voyage into an imaginary universe is colliding with imagery of a metabolic rebirth. Bits and pieces of promising luscious microclimates are scanned and unveiled before our very eyes by hypersensitive beams of light – or is it energy? - moving at a brisk pace in patterns alien to us.
Dreamrec

DIVISION (3) / (4) / (5)

Price: (3) 1.000 EUR / (4) 1.100 EUR / (5) 1.200 EUR as time is money

The fleeting, non-repetitive, intriguing nature of time is perfectly highlighted in this everchanging work aptly titled DIVISION. A virtual representation of a clock that in each minute generates a new aesthetic simulation based on the unique values of date and time notation, allows for, theoretically, endlessly unique images to be morphed every passing second.
